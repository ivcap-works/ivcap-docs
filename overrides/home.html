One Platform for the Full Research Lifecycle

Agentic & analytical workflows

Compose LLM-driven agents with traditional simulation, AI inference, and data-processing services into end-to-end pipelines — from exploratory research to automated, large-scale production runs.

Provenance, repeatability & accountability

The Data Fabric automatically records what data was used, which services processed it, and what results were produced — so you always know where an output came from and can reproduce it exactly.

Composable cross-disciplinary collaboration

Services built by different domain experts — ecologists, AI researchers, data engineers — can be freely combined into new workflows. Composability means a breakthrough in one field immediately becomes a building block for another.

From Prototype to Production — Without Starting Over

Package & publish services

Turn research code — notebooks, scripts, or models — into versioned, discoverable services that colleagues and automated pipelines can invoke at any scale.

Orchestrate agentic pipelines

Chain services into multi-step workflows driven by LLM agents or classic control logic. Scale from a single interactive run to ecosystem-wide batch processing without changing your code.

Trust your results with full provenance

Every artifact carries its full lineage — what data it came from, which service version produced it, and under what parameters — enabling robust audit trails and scientific reproducibility.
