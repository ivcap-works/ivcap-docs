## What is IVCAP?

IVCAP is a platform to **rapidly deploy and compose science/analytics services** so that researchers can tackle **far more ambitious ("100x") challenges** through collaboration across domains and organisations.

In IVCAP you:

- publish **services** (packaged analytics)
- submit **orders** (requests to run a service with parameters)
- produce **artifacts** (datasets, tables, models, images, reports)
- organise and discover data via the **Data Fabric**
- combine services into **composable workflows**

## Foundations (from the Jan 2024 platform overview)

1. **Composable workflows** – connect services and research steps into reproducible pipelines.
2. **Data Fabric** – manage records, collections, annotations, datasets, models, and observations.
3. **Dataset as query** – define datasets by intent (queries), e.g. “sample N images from camera C in geo-area G”.
